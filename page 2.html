Thunder Manifestation spotted at Amakumo Peak

There has been a sighting of a Thunder Manifestation at Amakumo Peak. It is strongly advised to avoid it and to evacuate the area surrounding it as it is dangerous with its lightning screens and its ability to make lightning fall anywhere. A bounty will be set on the monster at 500,000 mora and 1,600 primogems for whoever defeats it.
