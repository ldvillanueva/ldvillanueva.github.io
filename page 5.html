Best Support for your team!

Everyone can agree that Bennett is THE best support that anyone can get. He can heal so much hp and he can give an absolutely stacked damage boost based on your base attack. He gets better with constellations, his 1st constellation allowing him to give the damage boost without the need for your hp to be over 70%. His second constellation gives him 15 energy when he activates his burst. But you shouldn't fall for the 6th constellation, in most cases, he becomes unviable once you activate it since it infuses pyro in attacks from claymore, polearm, and sword characters. If you have a damage dealer that depends on a certain element that isn't pyro, it can ruin their damage immensely.

Best DPS for your team!

This is a character than everyone that plays the game can get for free. Xiangling, head chef of the Wanmin Restaurant definitely is the best dps that anyone can get. Her damage is everything a person needs from a dps, with her Pyronado and Guoba, she can deal massive damage. The best artifact set for her is definitely the new Emblem of Severed Fates, granting her energy recharge and extra elemental burst damage that scales from energy recharge. Her best weapon is The Catch which gives her MORE energy recharge and increases her Elemental Skill and burst damage by a lot, and it's even a free weapon!
